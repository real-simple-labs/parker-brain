# Product Launch — Process Index

The launch method — the scenarios, the newness taxonomy, the authority and proof doctrine — lives in `parker-system/creative-strategy-context/new-product-launch-creative.md`. Load it first; every process below assumes it. `strategy.md` classifies the launch and picks which of these run. These are the execution playbooks: how to do the step, not what the step means.

If a process and the launch doc disagree on what a scenario is, the launch doc wins.

## Always runs

- **diagnose-the-account** — The required pre-write diagnostic. Existing top performers, the closest existing SKU, the equity language worth reusing, the warm-audience plan, past winners *and* past failures, the product page, and any competitor refs in the brief. Most weak launch creative skips this step, and it shows in the output.

## One of these three, by scenario

- **scenario-a-depth-play** — Existing persona, new attribute or collection drop. Inherit the winning structures and swap the new element in. The risk is under-doing it until the launch goes invisible.
- **scenario-b-bridge-and-expand** — Existing persona, new format, occasion, use case, or bundle. The bridge from the hero product is the angle. Carries the bundle diagnosis.
- **scenario-c-audience-expansion** — New persona. Near-cold launch in the new audience's world, gated on persona work. Carries the persona mini-brief fallback for when no persona exists.

## Then, as the brief requires

- **proof-substitution** — Honest proof when the SKU has no reviews, no UGC, and no best-seller status yet. The substitution set, and the lines that must not be crossed.
- **launch-phase-sequencing** — Warm-audience recapture versus cold-audience scaling, and the pre-launch / launch-week / post-launch-evergreen arc. Keeps phase and awareness stage from collapsing into each other.
- **special-launch-contexts** — Gifting and occasion launches, giveaway-as-mechanic launches, and multi-video through-lines. These layer *on top of* the scenario playbook, never instead of it.
- **deliverable-specs** — The output spec for each Step Zero Question 4 deliverable type. Read this before producing anything, so a hooks brief does not get a storyboard.

## Before shipping

- **edge-cases-and-routing** — The substitution library for briefs that do not fit the clean path, plus the persona-fit check and the launch audit. Run the two checks on every launch regardless of scenario.
