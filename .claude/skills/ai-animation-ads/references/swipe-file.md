# Swipe File — Proven AI Animation Ad Scripts

Reference library of winning structures. Use for pattern inspiration — structural moves, voice, pacing — never copy lines. Tags: format · style · why it works.

## 1. Vue Skin — nose patches (talking-character cast · Pixar-style)
> (Nose, exasperated) "Can everyone stop attacking me for a second? I'm your nose and those dots you keep trying to get rid of? They're not blackheads."
> (Sebaceous Filament, cheeky) "Yeah, I'm not a blackhead. I'm a buildup of oil in your pore called a sebaceous filament. Your skin naturally fills me with oil. So even if you squeeze or strip me out, I'll just fill back up again."
> (Patch, calm hero) "That's why you need me. I'm a hydrocolloid nose patch. I gently absorb the oil from your nose pores… Just put me on whilst you sleep and I'll get to work… I don't pull or rip at your skin… So if you want clearer looking pores, stop trying to remove them and start gently managing the oil instead."

**Why it works:** myth-bust hook in the villain's own voice; the "it just fills back up" futility beat kills the old behavior; hero enters exactly at the turn; CTA is a reframe, not a pitch. Body-part + problem + product = complete 3-character cast.

> (Mouthwash, sheepish) "Don't worry, I got him. …I dried out your mouth and made it worse. I also only last about 20 minutes. My bad."

> (Device, muscular hero) "I use nano atomization. Same tech they use in medical devices. I kill the bacteria at the source, not cover it up like your $2 mouthwash and mint." → 3-second spray = 6 hours · "Trusted by 50,000 cigar guys who are tired of sleeping on the couch." · 90-day guarantee.

**Why it works:** THREE failed solutions each confess their specific failure mode — the funniest and most educational structure in the format; hyper-specific audience callout in the proof; a real-footage beat grounds the device at the turn (hybrid move).

In a healthy vein, tiny valves keep blood moving back up toward your heart. In a varicose vein, those valves have weakened… Blood slows, it pools, it sits there. Heavy legs by morning, aching by noon, shoes that don't fit by evening… Now imagine instead support from the outside. If your legs have been telling you something, this is what they've been saying. Look up Viasox compression socks."

**Why it works:** calm authority walking inside the body; the "morning → noon → evening" escalation makes an invisible problem hourly-concrete; "Now imagine instead" is a perfect one-line turn; the soft search CTA suits problem-aware cold traffic.

> (Old bathroom scale, assertive villain) "Your bathroom scale has been lying to you for years. I told you a number — that's all I do, that's all I've ever done." (Visceral fat character) "You'll never find me. Not with that thing. I've been growing for 8 weeks and nobody noticed."

## 9. Fragrance skeleton — 1800s (skeleton progression · origin/scenario + transformation)
Hook: "What would happen if you wore [scent] in the 1800s?" → skeleton sprays it in a steaming factory (workers stare) → cobblestone street, heads turn → a noblewoman swoons → apothecary marvels "at a fraction of the price" → payoff: skeleton crowned on a throne, "…their 30-day money-back guarantee."

**Why it works:** ordinary product + vivid era = instant novelty; social reactions escalate from recoil to worship (the benefit made visceral); guarantee lands as the punchline. The quality bar for skeleton ads, not a template.

## Reference mining with Parker (live swipes — run before scripting)

This file is the permanent pattern library; **Parker's ad library is the live one.** When the Parker MCP is connected, mine it at Stage 0–1 and cite the finds in the creative recommendation:

1. **Sweep the category for animation winners:** `search_competitor_facebook_ads {brandId, mode:'ads', keywords:["Pixar","claymation","animated character","cartoon","stop-motion","jingle"], sortBy:'impressions_rank', groupByMedia:true}`. Impressions rank ≈ sustained spend ≈ what's actually working. Scope with `brandNameSearch` for one competitor, `dateRangePreset:'last_30d'` for what's live right now, `relationshipType:'inspo'` for the creative-inspiration pool.
2. **Drill into 1–3 standouts only:** re-query those with `includeAdDetails:true` — each ad carries its full script, storyboard, audio notes, and analysis. A ranked ad's actual beat structure is the highest-value swipe material in the system; treat it exactly like the entries above (structural moves, never lines).
3. **Deconstruct a pasted reference:** when the user shares an ad URL, check `search_tiktok_videos` first (it may already be analyzed), else `analyze_video_from_url {videoUrl, notes:"deconstruct for format, beat structure, hook mechanics, visual style"}` — then rebuild through the pipeline for the user's brand.
4. **Mine the brand's own account:** `search_facebook_ads_semantic {brandId, query:"<angle or format>"}` — which angles and hooks already worked for THIS brand; winning static hooks feed video scripts, and vice versa.
5. **Log lineage:** note in the kit which reference ads shaped the structure (brand + Parker link) so the creative is reviewable and the swipe loop compounds.
6. **Pulling the reference IMAGE for anchoring:** competitor/inspo ad images live at `https://auth.heyparker.ai/storage/v1/object/public/internal-facebook-ads/external-brands/<externalBrandId>/<adArchiveId>-thumbnail.jpg`: this is the stable URL to `media_import_url`. The API's `imageUrl` field sometimes points at a non-existent path (404) and `thumbnailUrl` is a signed fbcdn link that expires; the pattern above is the one that works. Own-account ad images live at `.../internal-facebook-ads/<brandId>/<n>.jpg` as given in vault appendices. If all else fails, open the ad's `parkerWebUrl` in the browser and read the rendered `<img>` src from the DOM.

Without Parker connected: use this file plus any swipe links the user provides, and say that live-library mining was skipped.

**How to use the entries above:** before scripting, pull the 2–3 matching the chosen format and note the structural moves (not lines) — then let the Parker sweep tell you how those moves are being executed in the brand's category this month.
