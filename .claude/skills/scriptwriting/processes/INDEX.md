# Scriptwriting — Process Index

Three groups. The default adaptation path runs first. The idea paths are the distinct ways a strategist arrives at a script idea from the surfaces the brand vaults hold — pick the one whose source surface is live. The framework fallbacks are net-new from scratch, used only when no surface and no reference exist.

Every process's output step cites the voice audit. The human-voice doctrine and the brand voice-profile method live at `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/spoken-script-voice.md` — read it before writing any script's words, and run the brand voice profile against the draft before output.

## The default adaptation path

The standard flow. Build the brand's voice profile first, find the strongest reference, adapt 1:1.

- **build-brand-voice-profile** — Run first. Build or load the brand's spoken-voice profile per `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/spoken-script-voice.md` — pacing, diction, signature moves, direct-address habit, CTA shape, and the banned-by-absence list, from the brand's own winning scripts read out loud. The profile is the brand baseline and the voice audit every other process's output is checked against.
- **find-reference-ads** — Pull a handful of reference ads via Parker MCP in source order: the brand's own account scripts first, then captured vault competitor/inspo scripts, then the niche organic library, then the global AI-tagged DB. Filter by the AI tags that match the request. Picks the single strongest candidate.
- **adapt-existing-script** — Adapt the chosen reference 1:1. Preserves the structural skeleton; rewrites every word in the brand's voice fingerprint.

## The idea paths

Every distinct way a strategist arrives at a script idea from the vault. Each line names the path and the vault source surface that fires it.

- **script-from-winning-ad** — A NEW script from the brand's own winner DNA: proven hook on a new body, proven body re-opened, two validated pieces stitched. Fires when the brand has an ad with real performance signal and the goal is new, not a variant. Source: the brand's winning ads. Cross-routes to the iterations skill for variants of one winner.
- **script-from-competitor-ad** — Adapt a captured rival via target inversion: same skeleton, the buyer they shame becomes the buyer we absolve, the claim they can't make becomes ours. Fires when a competitor ad has signal and a defensible counter-position exists. Source: `prompts/competitor-profile/competitor-ad-account-evaluation.md` and single-ad teardowns.
- **script-from-organic-niche** — Adapt a breakout organic video to paid, with the honesty rule on hacks. Fires when a niche organic has view and engagement signal. Source: the `search_and_manage_organic_social` MCP surface and `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/organic-social-analysis.md`.
- **script-from-review** — A golden-nugget review is the spine: the verbatim is the hook, the reviewer's trigger-doubt-verdict arc is the beat structure. Fires when a review has a clean dramatizable arc. Source: `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/customer-review-mining-method.md` and `prompts/voice-of-customer/`.
- **script-from-comment-thread** — A real comment read on camera and answered; the comment IS the hook. Fires on the dupe warning, the price objection, the skeptic. Source: `prompts/personas/ad-comments.md` and the ad-comments MCP surfaces.
- **script-from-voc-convergence** — A VoC convergence is the script's whole grammar; the script speaks only in the converged vocabulary. Fires when a phrase or image converges across many mentions. Source: `prompts/voice-of-customer/voice-of-customer-assembly.md`.
- **script-from-persona-lifecycle** — Pick the persona, pick the journey stage, write in that persona's documented diction at that moment. Fires for win-back, gifting, post-purchase reassurance. Source: `prompts/personas/persona-voice-library.md` and `prompts/personas/lifecycle-journey-maps.md`.
- **script-from-validated-insight** — A validated hypothesis is the claim spine; the script dramatizes the research finding. Fires when the open-loops pipeline validates a finding. Source: `prompts/open-loops/open-loops-roll-up.md`.
- **script-from-open-lane** — Whitespace-driven: the gaps doc names an empty lane and the script is built into it. Fires on a named open lane with evidence — the absolution register, the problem-mirror, the explanation hook. Source: `prompts/market-synthesis/gaps-opportunities-inspo.md`.
- **script-from-idea-bank** — Promote a logged idea through concepting into a script; the justification field is the reason to exist. Fires when concepting from the bank. Source: `prompts/ideas-and-briefs/brand-idea-bank.md`, tied to `prompts/ideas-and-briefs/brief-creation.md`.
- **script-from-customer-ritual** — Film the ritual the community already performs, honestly. Fires when a customer-invented demo is documented — the squat test, the drain check, the belt hack. Source: `prompts/personas/customer-reviews.md` and the review-mining method.
- **script-from-calendar-moment** — A seasonal or campaign moment in the brand's own MO: days-out promo flashes, gifting in the buyer's voice, launch beats. Fires when a documented calendar moment approaches. Source: `prompts/brand-profile/marketing-calendar-and-campaigns.md`.
- **script-from-founder-story** — The origin/why-narrative within what's publicly verified; routes to intake when the founder is absent from canon. Fires for founder-led, unaware or problem-aware placements. Source: `prompts/brand-profile/operations-and-team.md`.
- **script-from-authority** — A credentialed expert within the claims envelope; the lived moment beats the lab coat. Fires when a real documented expert backs the brand. Source: `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/expert-insights/INDEX.md` and `prompts/global-databases/expert-signal-db.md`.

## The framework fallbacks

Net-new from scratch. Used only when no surface above is live and no reference exists.

- **write-problem-solution-script** — Problem-agitate-benefit. For problem/solution brands, problem-aware or solution-aware audiences.
- **write-storytelling-script** — Organic story-arc (intro → conflict → discovery → resolution). For unaware audiences, founder content, longer-form, lifestyle brands.
- **write-vsl** — Full VSL pipeline: hook + lead archetype + mechanism + close.
- **write-awareness-stage-script** — Script built around a specific awareness stage.
- **write-sophistication-matched-script** — High-sophistication (acknowledge failed attempts) vs low-sophistication (educate on the problem).
- **write-style-based-script** — A named style: humorous, testimonial, demonstration, FUD, educational.
- **write-emotional-depth-script** — Mapped to a level of emotional depth: surface, behavioral, identity.

## Sub-processes

- **build-vsl-mechanism** — Builds the Million-Dollar Mechanism (UMP, UMS, delivery system). Called inside write-vsl.
- **write-cta-natural** — CTA builder. Writes the close in conversational, recommendation-style language. Called at the end of any scripting process.
