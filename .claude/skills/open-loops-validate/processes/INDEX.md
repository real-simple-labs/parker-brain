# Open-loops-validate processes

The three sub-procedures the skill calls. The main flow is in `SKILL.md`; these are the parts worth their own page.

| Process | When it runs | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **resolve-the-state** | Step 4, after the plan and reverse-evidence are in | The four-state decision rubric. Measures the evidence against the hypothesis's pre-set bar and lands the loop in validated, invalidated, inconclusive, or insufficient — and draws the line between the two soft states. |
| **the-closure-document** | Step 5–6, after the state is decided | The closure-doc spec, where it files by state, and what it propagates: validated insights into the brand profile + recent-validations, invalidated into a known dead-end, soft states held in the validations tree. |
| **re-validation** | Step 7, when a loop validates | Stamps freshness and a re-check date, and defines how a scheduled re-validation re-runs the test and updates, retires, or re-confirms the insight. |
