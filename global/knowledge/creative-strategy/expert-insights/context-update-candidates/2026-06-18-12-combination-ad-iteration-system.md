# Context update candidates — The 12-combination ad-iteration system (Sarah)

## Candidate summary

This source is an operator-educator's framework for iterating winning ads: hold the angle fixed and vary only *valence/intensity* (four zones) and *self* (actual/ideal/ought), which collapses to a finite **12-combination grid** for how any one message can be communicated. It *operationalizes* a prior Parker already half-holds — "psychological diversity" as an iteration/scaling lever from the two June-3 signals — by giving it a named, attributable taxonomy plus a diagnostic discipline (encode zone+self in ad names so wins attribute to communication, not format/creator/offer).

It should not rewrite canonical iteration or scriptwriting method from one self-reported framework with no account data. It should land as named candidate additions to `iterations.md`, `scriptwriting.md`, `creative-strategy-fundamentals.md`, and a corroborating note near the brand-brain CLAUDE template. The performance reads are inferred from ad-library run dates, and the zone/self labels are explicitly subjective.

## Proposed context updates

**Target path:** `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/iterations.md`
**Proposed change:** Add an iteration axis: hold the angle/message/hypothesis fixed and remix only the *communication* — valence/intensity (Zone 1 low-positive, Zone 2 high-positive, Zone 3 low-negative, Zone 4 high-negative) and self (actual / ideal / ought) — yielding up to 12 named combinations. Frame it as a structured complement to the doc's existing iteration moves: when an ad is winning, one high-confidence path is to keep the angle and move it through adjacent psychological cells.
**Evidence:** Source: "I could only really test 12 combinations of how I'm communicating this message"; live teardown of a long-running Norse Organics winner read as Zone 3/4 → Zone 1, actual-self hero's journey.

**Evidence:** Source: "every single ad we run towards zone one ideal is crushing... this way we don't get stuck thinking it's the format... or just that creator."

### 3. Scriptwriting — zone coherence as a script/edit rule
**Target path:** `global/knowledge/creative-strategy/scriptwriting.md` (and `spoken-script-voice.md` for the audio half)
**Proposed change:** Add a rule: at any moment, the visual, the audio/music, and the copy should carry the *same* emotional zone; and the arc should hold a zone long enough for the viewer to cross it before transitioning. **Evidence:** Source: the broken Norse ad pairs a Zone 2 hype voice with a Zone 4 panic message; the winner stays in a zone long enough and sequences 3/4 → 1.
**Confidence:** mixed
**Promotion condition:** Apply at next scriptwriting refresh; aligns with the doc's existing "people buy on emotion" and clarity-over-cleverness posture.
