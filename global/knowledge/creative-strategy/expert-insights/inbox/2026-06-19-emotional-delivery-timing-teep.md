# Expert signal — Emotional delivery and timing (valence/intensity + TEEP)

## Source read

An article arguing that sophisticated creative teams hit a ceiling not because of a creative problem but a *layering* problem: they generate emotion but ignore how it *lands* — the emotional state the viewer is already in, and where she is in her internal decision. Two diagnostics: (1) **valence × intensity** (same grid we already hold), with the note that low-intensity-positive / Zone 1 is the underinvested mid-funnel quadrant; (2) **T-E-E-P** — Trigger → Exploration → Evaluation → Purchase, the *internal decision state* (distinct from funnel/awareness), each phase able to receive a different message. Plus the emotional-landing-state mechanism (high arousal narrows attention + drives action but suppresses identity-forming reflection; low arousal expands attention + engages the default-mode network → identification), the funnel-as-emotional-arc, and 7 tactics (audits, the TEEP brief rewrite, pacing-as-cognition, context-vs-intent, retarget-around-the-internal-conversation).
