# Open-loops-advance processes

The two sub-procedures the skill calls. The main flow is in `SKILL.md`; these are the parts worth their own page.

| Process | When it runs | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| **build-the-test-plan** | Step 4, after the prediction is written | Builds a closeable plan: owned data first, size-first when the loop is about an opportunity, the mandatory reverse-evidence step, and the evidence bar set before any research runs. Names when a loop cannot be validated. |
| **the-approval-gate** | Step 5, before the hypothesis is filed | Decides whether Parker runs the hypothesis on its own or routes it to the user. Brand-routed and novel-high-stakes loops go to `awaiting-user`; everything else proceeds on Parker's latitude. |
