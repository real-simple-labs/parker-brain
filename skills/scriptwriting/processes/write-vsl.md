# Process — Write VSL (Video Sales Letter)

Full long-form video sales letter pipeline. 90 seconds to several minutes. The structure is hook + lead archetype + unique mechanism + close. Used for high-consideration purchases and complex product education.

## Required inputs

- Brand profile, ICP, voice of customer.
- The product's mechanism — the specific reason it works.
- Proof points — clinical studies, testimonials, case studies, credentials.
- The offer — price, guarantee, bonus structure, urgency element.
- Format runtime target.

## When to pick

- **Best for:** high-consideration purchases. Complex products requiring education. Premium and high-ticket offers. Categories where customer journey is long. Top-of-funnel awareness through bottom-of-funnel conversion.
- **Worst for:** impulse categories. Quick-conversion brands. Audiences with short attention.

## VSL pipeline structure

1. **Hook (0-10 seconds).** Demographic callout + open loop. Investment hook, scam hook, or contrarian claim work well at this length.
2. **Lead archetype (10-30 seconds).** One of the named lead archetypes — discovery story, problem-aware lead, false-hope lead, hidden-villain lead. Pick based on awareness stage.
3. **Problem deep-dive (30-60 seconds).** Agitate the pain. Go to Level 2 and Level 3 emotional depth. Acknowledge the failed past attempts if the audience is solution-aware.
4. **Mechanism reveal (60-100 seconds).** The unique mechanism. Why this works when other things have not. Use the build-vsl-mechanism sub-process to build this specifically.
5. **Proof stack (100-150 seconds).** Testimonials, clinical results, before-and-after, credentials. Stack multiple proof elements.
6. **Offer (150-180 seconds).** Price, value stack, bonuses, anchors. The complete offer.
7. **Close (180-end).** Guarantee, urgency, scarcity. Risk reversal. Final CTA.

## Execution

1. **Lock the awareness stage first.** The lead archetype depends on it. Unaware → storytelling lead. Problem-aware → educational lead. Solution-aware → false-hope or hidden-villain lead.
2. **Build the unique mechanism.** Call build-vsl-mechanism as a sub-step. The mechanism is the differentiator that justifies the price and the claim.
3. **Pace the proof stack.** Multiple proof types stacked — qualitative (testimonials) and quantitative (clinical, ratings). Each proof beat is 10-15 seconds.
4. **Frame the offer with anchors.** Price comparisons to what the audience would otherwise spend. Value stack of what is included. Guarantee.
5. **Close with risk reversal.** Money-back guarantee, free shipping, free returns — whatever removes the buyer's perceived risk.

## Output content

- The full VSL script with timing per beat.
- Visual cues per beat.
- The lead archetype chosen and why.
- The mechanism reveal section in detail.
- The offer structure.
- The CTA.

## What never to do

- VSL for impulse categories where the audience does not need a long sales letter.
- Skip the mechanism. The audience needs to understand WHY this works.
- Generic proof. Specific testimonials, specific stats, specific clinical results.
- Multiple CTAs. One offer, one CTA.
