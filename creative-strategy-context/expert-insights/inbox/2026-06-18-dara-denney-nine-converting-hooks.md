# Expert signal — Dara Denney's nine "converting" Meta hooks (anti-bloat verdict)

## Why this signal exists but mostly does not get promoted

Jimmy's standing rule: hooks.md is already 1,200+ lines and is loaded whole at hook-write time, so adding near-duplicate formats makes generation *worse*, not better. Saved here as provenance (the inbox is never loaded when writing hooks, so it is zero bloat to the generation path); the canonical doc is left alone by default.

## The nine, mapped against the existing taxonomy

1. **Trigger word ("ruining my life")** — already `hooks.md` #14. Her move (run top-5 hooks through an LLM, inject trigger words into a structure already converting) is an iteration workflow, not a new format.
2. **"Why is it important"** — overlaps Educational (#16) + Question (#20). Specific opener phrasing for a value prop; not a distinct format.
3. **"A day without pain" (lead with transformation, invert problem→solution)** — overlaps Speed & Transformation (#9) + Storytelling (#18). A script-structure note, not a hook type.
4. **How-to (know if / pick / start)** — #8 "How Do I Know If" exists. The funnel-stage siblings are the one genuinely additive, non-ephemeral item: *how do I pick/choose* = decision/solution-aware stage; *how to start* = activation/most-aware (good for service businesses). Worth at most a one-line sharpen of #8.
5. **"If you" hook** — already Demographic (#6); "If you're between 25-35…" is a verbatim example there.
6. **"This one thing" (simplicity promise)** — overlaps Educational + Question; minor opener variant.
7. **Apology / "you're welcome"** — not in the doc, but the source itself calls it a "cheap trick… bottom of the barrel." Not worth canonizing.
8. **Snapchat-filter visual hook** — net-new but a platform-trend execution; ephemeral. Swipe/patterns-to-monitor, not canon.
9. **Whisper/ASMR sound hook (top voiceover → whisper)** — net-new, concrete sound-hook tactic; somewhat ephemeral. Swipe/patterns-to-monitor.

## The non-hook insight worth more than the hooks

Her testing point: take *big swings* in variation testing — do not change one word to "isolate the variable"; reformat your top-5 ads across all these hooks as one big test (3–6 hook variations per test). This is genuinely useful but it is testing/iteration method and belongs on the iterations surface, not in hooks.md.
